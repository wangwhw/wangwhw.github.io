Welcome! My name is Wang Wenhui William and I am a PhD Candidate at the Department of Geography, The University of Hong Kong

My research interests lie in tourism geography. I've researched how banks cause credit frictions to the real economy, how they interact with regulators and how their CEOs/CFOs words can be used to proxy the uncertainty banks face. Helping me along the way, I've been learning how to apply recent techniques in machine learning and natural language processing to these economic problems. Feel free to look at my research and my tutorials to learn more!
